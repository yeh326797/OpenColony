Services for Companies

The future of work is a networked economy where innovation is human centered and operationally driven. We help companies hire, grow, acquire, and partner with the industry’s best.

Advisory

We fill a critical gap by delivering on-demand experts when hiring isn’t convenient or ultimately feasible. Advisory gives companies the resources they need to make necessary, complex, and time-sensitive decisions without the carrying cost of a pricey hire they may not be able to withstand. Read More

As the pace of innovation continues to ratchet up, shoring up the right leaders in new fields is the core challenge, especially in relation to the larger value chain of innovation, production, and operations. New disciplines, tools, technologies, and systems inevitably fragment and scatter—Advisory is our salve.

Top people are the exclusive irreplaceable advantage, with the best organizations in the world being well designed, self-sustaining human operating systems. The human factor is not just the greatest advantage but potentially the trickiest factor in managing a business of any size. Inherent in the reality of human capital is that humans are the capital, not the companies. Their value shines when they’re engaged in a humanistic, considered context that supports their values, interests, and ambitions.

Advisory reflects Open Colony’s customers’ needs: individuals at the top of their career arc hungry for opportunities to apply their experience and make an impact. At the same time, companies increasingly request targeted, curated access to The Colony for strategic guidance, business development, specialty advisory boards, domain intelligence, as well as search and leadership development.
